# Security Policy

Thank you for helping keep EasyCo and our users safe!

## Reporting Security Issues

If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it to us at:
- Email: security@easyco.be
- Please do NOT file a public issue

## Our Commitment

- We will respond to your report within 48 hours
- We will keep you informed of the progress
- We will credit you for your discovery (if desired)

## Scope

In scope:
- Authentication and authorization issues
- SQL injection, XSS, CSRF vulnerabilities
- Sensitive data exposure
- Security misconfigurations
- Rate limiting bypass

Out of scope:
- Social engineering attacks
- Physical security issues
- Denial of Service (DoS) attacks
- Issues in third-party services

## Safe Harbor

We consider security research conducted consistent with this policy to be:
- Authorized concerning any applicable anti-hacking laws
- Exempt from DMCA takedown notices
- Exempt from restrictions in our Terms of Service

Thank you for helping us maintain the security of EasyCo!
